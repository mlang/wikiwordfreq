Word frequency counter

Taken from full wikipedia dumps.

Three lists are provided per language, all are sorted by frequency (descending):

known
A list of known (according to a wordlist) words.
unknown
Words which do not appear in the wordlist for the particular language.
known-subseq
All common subsequences of known words.
  • de (German)
    • known words
    • known word substrings
  • en (English)
    • known words
    • known word substrings
